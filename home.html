Why LeBron is better than Jordan

  1. Longevity
    • Has played 21 seasons to Jordan's 15
    • Has over 40,000 points scored in his career to MJ's 32,292
    • Averaged 25.7 points, 7.3 rebounds, and 8.3 assists in his 21st season
  2. Harder Era
    • Players today are more skilled than when Jordan played
    • Revolution of the 3 point line has led to better offenses
    • Faster paced games
  3. Less Help
    • Jordan had hall of famer Scottie Pippen for each of his 6 championships
    • His team was able to build a better team because Jordan stayed with the Bulls most of his career
    • LeBron was forced to adapt to many different teams due to him changing teams frequently
